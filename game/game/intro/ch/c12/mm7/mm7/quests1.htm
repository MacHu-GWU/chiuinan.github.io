光明路線任務

主線任務
任務名稱交付者報酬完成方式
Enter Celeste from the grand teleporter in the Bracada Desert, then talk to Gavin Magnus in Castle Lambent in Celeste. Harmondale的新任仲裁官 Celeste的Castle Lambent 選擇光明後，新的仲裁官給你的任務，要你去面見光明領袖Gavin Magnus。
Complete the Walls of Mist without killing a single opponent and return to Gavin Magnus in Castle Lambent in Celeste. Celeste的Castle Lambent Celeste的Walls of Mist 光明領袖給你的第一個任務，證明你的實力。你只要進入Walls of Mist，不殺任何一隻怪物，找齊三把鑰匙，然後從開啟的出口離開即可。完成後，光明領袖會請你去見他的四個顧問，繼續下一步的任務。
Retrieve the altar piece from the Temple of Light in Celeste and the Temple of Dark in the Pit and return them to Resurectra in Castle Lambent in Celeste. Celeste(10)的Resurectra Celeste的Temple of Light
The Pit的Temple of Dark 		光明系四個顧問任務之一。找到在光明和黑暗神殿裡面的祭壇碎片(Altar Piece)回報即可。
Investigate the Wine Cellar in Tatalia and return to Crag Hack in Castle Lambent in Celeste. Celeste(11)的Crag Hack Tatalia的Wine Cellar 光明系四個顧問任務之一。殺死酒窖內的所有怪物和吸血鬼後回報即可。酒窖裡面有幾個小秘室，請注意務必全部清光。
Retrieve the Case of Soul Jars from Castle Gloaming in the Pit and return to Sir Caneghem in Celeste. Celeste(12)的Sir Caneghem The Pit的Castle Gloaming 光明系四個顧問任務之一。到The Pit的Castle Gloaming裡面尋找Soul Jars後回報即可。
Assassinate Tolberti in his house in the Pit and return his control cube to Robert the Wise in Celeste. Celeste(9)的Robert the Wise The Pit(17)的Tolberti's House 光明系四個顧問任務之一，需完成前面三個顧問任務才能接到。到The Pit的Tolberti's House幹掉Tolberti拿到他身上的Control Cube回報即可。Tolberti相當難纏，不僅血長還會補血，幹掉他同時可以得到一把能源槍。回報之後,可以習得Blaster的武器技能，它的Expert和Master在另外兩個顧問那可以學到，Grandmaster則要到Castle Lambent找Resurectra學了。
Go to Colony Zod in the Land of the Giants and slay Xenofex then return to Resurectra in Castle Lambent in Celeste. Castle Lambent的Resurectra Land of Giants的Colony Zod 完成四個顧問任務之後，最後一個顧問會要求你到Celeste Lambent找Resurectra。Resurectra會要求你去Colony Zod殺了惡魔之王Xenofex，殺了Xenofex後回報即可。
Go to the Lincoln in the sea west of Avlee and retrieve the Oscillation Overthruster and return it to Resurectra in Celeste. Castle Lambent的Resurectra Shoals的The Lincoln 這是遊戲最後一個任務，你必須穿上在覲見室外寶箱中找到的潛水衣，潛入Avlee西邊Shoals海底的林肯號，找回震盪加速器(Oscillation Overthruster)。找到之後回報Resurectra，就可以看到遊戲結束畫面囉。

任務名稱交付者報酬完成方式
Go to the Mercenary Guild in Tatalia and talk to Niles Stantley within two weeks. 選擇光明系一個月後回到Harmondale大陸時 Tatalia的Mercenary Guild 在Emerald Island如果有接受Stantley的火球法杖，才會接到這個任務。兩星期內要去Tatalia的Mercenary Guild找Stantley，否則Stantley會率眾攻擊Harmondale。
轉職任務
任務名稱交付者報酬完成方式
Purify the Altar of Evil in the Temple of the Moon on Evenmorn Isle then return to Rebecca Devine in Celeste. Celeste(14)的Rebecca Devine Evenmorn Isle的Temple of the Moon Priest轉職成Priest of Light的任務。到月神殿的祭壇前按一下鍵就完成淨化神殿的任務了。
Go to Watchtower 6 in the Deyja Moors, and move the weight from the top of the tower to the bottom of the tower. Then return to William Lasker in the Erathian Sewers. Erathian Sewer(2)的William Lasker Deyja Moors的Watchtower 6 Rogue轉職成Spy的任務。到WatchTower 6頂層南邊房間中移動一下平衡錘即可。
Retrieve the bones of the Dwarf King from the tunnels Stone City and Nighon and place them in their proper resting place in the Barrow Downs, then return to Anthony Green in the Tularean Forest. Tularean Forest(29)的Anthony Green Barrow Downs的Nighon Tunnel及Zokarr's Tomb Great Druid轉職成Arch Druid的任務。在Nighon Tunnel東北角落可以找到Zokarr's Skull，將骷髏頭放回到Zokarr's Tomb棺木中回報即可。
Rescue Alice Hargreaves from William's Tower in the Deyja Moors then talk to Sir Charles Quixote. Erathia(16)的Sir Charles Quixote Deyja Moors的William's Tower Crusader轉職成Hero的任務。Deyja Moors(19)位置的小屋，在選擇光明系之後便會變成William's Tower城堡，Alice就在城堡頂層北邊上鎖的房間中等待救援，帶著她回報即可。
Retrieve the Perfect Bow from the Titans' Stronghold in Avlee and return it to Lawrence Mark in Harmondale. Harmondale(4)的Lawrence Mark Avlee的Titans' Stronghold Warrior Mage轉職成Master Archer的任務。Titans' Stronghold中有一狗票的龍和泰坦巨人，難度極高，若真打不過請用隱身術進入，然後到入口西北方向小房間中竊取Perfect Bow回報即可。
Go to the Temple of Baa in Avlee and kill the High Priest of Baa, then return to Bartholomew Hume in Harmondale. Harmondale(28)的Bartholomew Hume Avlee的Temple of Baa Initiate轉職成Master的任務。到Temple of Baa東北房間中殺掉High Priest of Baa即可回報。
Win five arena challenges then return to Leda Rowan in the Barcada Desert. Barcada Desert(21)的Leda Rowan Arena Cavalier轉職成Champion的任務。星期天從Harmondale的馬房出發到Arena，贏得五場Knight級的勝利即可回報。
Find the Book of Divine Intervention in the Breeding Zone in the Pit and return it to Thomas Grey in the School of Sorcery. Bracada Desert的School of Sorcery裡邊的Thomas Grey The Pit的Breeding Zone Wizard轉職成Archmage的任務。到The Pit的Breeding Zone裡邊尋找光系終極法術書回報即可，你還可以留一份副本自己唸。注意的是，在Breeding Zone裡面殺怪物是沒有經驗的，所以，不用戀戰，隱形或閃避進去拿到書就可以閃了。
Calm the trees in the Tularean Forest by speaking to the Oldest Tree then return to Lysader Sweet in the Bracada Desert. Bracada Desert(22)的Lysader Sweet Tularean Forest東邊小島的Oldest Tree Hunter轉職成Ranger Lord的任務。配合支線任務第一項到Tatalia的Mercenary Guild找回樹之心，將它還給在Tularean Forest上的古老之樹便能安撫樹精，成功歸還樹之心後即可回報。
支線任務
任務名稱交付者報酬完成方式
Find the Heart of the Forest in the Mercenary Guild in Tatalia and return it to the Oldest Tree in the Tularean Forest. Tularean Forest東邊小島的Oldest Tree Tatalia的Mercenary Guild 這是Hunter轉職成Ranger Lord任務的過程。到Tatalia的Mercenary Guild找到樹之心，將他拿給Tularean Forest東邊小島的Oldest Tree即可。
Steal the Tapestry from your associate's Castle and return it to Niles Stantley in the Mercenary Guild in Tatalia. Tatalia的Mercenary Guild Celeste的Castle Lambent 接到上個訊息後兩個星期內有到Tatalia的Mercenary Guild找Stantley的話，就會接到這個任務，要你在一個月內到Celeste的Castle Lambent去偷取一張掛毯。如果你偷了，城堡內的守衛會與你為敵一個月，若是你不去偷，一個月後Stantley會率眾攻擊Harmondale。
Retrieve the Seasons' Stole from the Hall of the Pit and return it to Gary Zimm in the Bracada Desert. Bracada Desert(6)的Gary Zimm Deyja Moors的Hall of the Pit 在Hall of the Pit接近The Pit出口旁的寶箱中會找到這件物品，把他交還給Gary Zimm即可。
